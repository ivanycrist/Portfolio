How I work

My approach connects numbers, people, and processes:

  • I listen and observe: understand the associate’s context, customer needs, and operational constraints before suggesting change.
  • I translate data into stories: use metrics like CSAT, AHT, FCR, and error rates to explain what is happening and why.
  • I coach with clarity: give specific, respectful feedback that agents can apply on their next interaction.
  • I document and follow up: create reports, action plans, and trackers that show progress over time.
  • I align with leadership: work with Training and Operations to embed quality into processes, not only into scripts.

I look for teams that want a Quality Analyst who is comfortable in both data and human conversations: someone who can monitor calls, coach agents, and speak the language of leaders and clients.
