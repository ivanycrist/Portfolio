How I work

My approach combines structure and humanity:

  • I listen first: understand context, emotions, and urgency from the customer or internal stakeholder.
  • I clarify the map: make the process visible (what happened, what will happen, what options exist) with simple examples.
  • I act with precision: follow the process, but I am willing to investigate off-script when the customer needs it.
  • I leave useful traces: clear documentation, summaries, and notes that make the next handoff easy.
  • I propose improvements: if something creates repeated friction, I turn it into insight to adjust metrics, guides, or flows.

I look for roles where I can bring this blend of QA, Customer Support, and continuous improvement mindset to teams that value customer experience.
